# Hookapedia Image Style Guide

This document defines the visual style and prompt structure for generating recipe images for Hookapedia. Consistency in imagery is key to a premium user experience.

## Core Aesthetic
- **Style:** Professional Macro Food Photography / Product Shot.
- **Composition:** Glass bowl filled with fresh ingredients, ice, and mint.
- **Atmosphere:** Dark textured background (slate/stone), dramatic lighting.
- **Key Element:** Swirling colorful smoke (pink, green, blue) surrounding the bowl.
- **Resolution/Quality:** Hyper-realistic, raw photo quality, sharp focus, natural textures.

## Prompt Structure
When generating images for new recipes, use the following template:

```text
Professional macro photography of a glass bowl filled with [INGREDIENTS_LIST], ice cubes, and fresh mint leaves. Swirling colorful smoke surrounding the bowl. Dark textured slate background, dramatic cinematic lighting, vibrant colors, hyper-realistic, raw photo, sharp focus, highly detailed texture, 8k resolution.
```

### Components:
1.  **Subject:** "Professional photography of a hookah bowl mix..." - establishes the context.
2.  **Ingredients:** "[INGREDIENTS_LIST]" - e.g., "vanilla flowers, caramel drizzle, and fresh mint leaves". List the key visual flavor components.
    *   *Tip:* Use visual representations of flavors (e.g., "sliced grapefruit" instead of just "grapefruit flavor").
3.  **Atmosphere:** "Dark moody background, cinematic lighting" - ensures the premium dark mode aesthetic.
4.  **Technical Specs:** "photorealistic, 8k resolution" - ensures high quality.
5.  **Detail:** "Hookah shisha tobacco texture visible" - adds realism specific to the product.

## Example
**Recipe:** Vanilla-Caramel with Mint
**Prompt:**
> Professional photography of a hookah bowl mix featuring vanilla flowers, caramel drizzle, and fresh mint leaves. Dark moody background, cinematic lighting, photorealistic, 8k resolution. Hookah shisha tobacco texture visible.
